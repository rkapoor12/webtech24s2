Project Proposal

Objective of my website

The project objective will be to showcase my travel experiences and personal reviews of the places that I have visited. The website will allow users to look at various sites that I recommend that they should visit in each place. This will be demonstrated with a sample of three cities/places that I have visited with pictures that I have taken during the trip as well.

Target Audience of my website

My website is to help travel enthusiasts learn more about tourism opportunities in Indonesia and Spain. I hope to encourage them to travel to these places through providing them with a guide of what they can do while they are visiting.

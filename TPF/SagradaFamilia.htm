La Sagrada Familia

La Sagrada Familia is an iconic basilica in Barcelona, Spain, renowned for its breathtaking architecture and intricate design. Designed by the visionary architect Antoni Gaudí, construction of this UNESCO World Heritage site began in 1882 and continues to this day. Its towering spires, ornate facades, and stunning stained glass windows make it one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.

My family and I visited La Sagrada Familia in August of 2023. Seeing the landmark was an amazing experience. The insides of the basilica were lined with stained glass windows in vibrant hues, casting a mesmerizing kaleidoscope of colors throughout the interior. The architecture throughout the church was especially detailed with every curvature having a unique design. It truly felt like stepping into a living work of art!

I would definitely highly recommend visiting La Sagrada Familia. It is definitely a must visit sight if you go to Barcelona!
    Travel Tips:
    1. Barcelona is known to have pick pocketers especially at a popular site such as La Sagrada Familia. Be sure to keep your belongings safe and keep an eye on them.
    2. Although you usually need to pay to get into La Sagrada Familia, you can visit it for free if you go during Sunday Mass.
    3. The church is quite small and there isn’t a lot to do so around an hour should be enough during your day to visit the Church.

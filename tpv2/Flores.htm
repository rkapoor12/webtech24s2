Flores

Welcome to Flores, an enchanting Indonesian island paradise where natural wonders await at every turn. Let's embark on a journey to discover some of its most breathtaking attractions.

First up, we have Padar Island, a hidden gem known for its dramatic landscapes and panoramic views. As we hike to the island's highest peak, you can see lush green hills, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters.

Next, we set sail for Pink Beach, a rare and stunning stretch of shoreline known for its unique pink-hued sands. And no trip to Flores would be complete without a visit to Komodo Dragon Island, home to the legendary Komodo dragons.

Pink Beach and Padar Island

Pink Beach is a stunning natural wonder located on Komodo Island in Indonesia. What sets Pink Beach apart from other beaches around the world is its unique pink-colored sand, a result of the mixing of white sand with red coral fragments and microscopic organisms called Foraminifera.

The beach is also renowned for its vibrant underwater ecosystem, offering excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities for visitors to explore colorful coral reefs teeming with diverse marine life. I did some snorkeling which I really enjoyed. I saw over five manta rays which was the most amazing experience.

Another site close to Komodo Dragon Island is aPadar Island. Padar Island boasts breathtaking panoramic views from its mountainous terrain, offering hiking trails that lead to spectacular viewpoints overlooking the surrounding turquoise waters and rugged coastline. However, to reach these viewpoints requires hiking up at leas 700 steps! However, it is definitely worth it and I would highly suggest it.

    Travel Tip:
    1. Wear athletic shoes as you need proper footwear for hiking
    2. Carry lots of water
    3. Carry sunscreen and wear a cap to prevent overheating and tanning
    4. Carry only a few items as you do not want anything excess to weigh you down
